# Privacy Policy for ScreenSwap

*Last updated: May 1, 2026*

ScreenSwap is a free, open source utility. It does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data.

## What the app accesses

* **Running windows and their positions** — to move them between monitors
* **Keyboard input** — to detect configured global shortcuts
* **Display configuration** — to enumerate connected monitors

Keyboard input is processed locally and is never recorded, stored, or transmitted.

## Data storage

The only data stored is your own settings (shortcut assignments, animation duration, main monitor preference), saved locally to your Windows user profile using standard application settings.

This data never leaves your device.
